# Pomote v1.0.0

The first public release of Pomote, a quiet native Pomodoro timer for the macOS menu bar.

## Highlights

- Native, lightweight AppKit menu bar app
- Automatic Pomodoro focus and break cycles
- Configurable focus and break durations
- Visual, silent notification, and sound reminders
- Chinese and English interface
- Universal support for Apple Silicon and Intel Macs

Download `Pomote-1.0.0.dmg`, open it, and drag Pomote into Applications.

Pomote is ad-hoc signed and not yet notarized. macOS may require first-launch confirmation in System Settings > Privacy & Security.
